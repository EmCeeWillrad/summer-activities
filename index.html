Vermont Guest Guide

South Hero

Restaurants

  • Wally’s Place – Local favorite for bagels and breakfast sandwiches
  • Seb’s Snack Bar – Seasonal drive-up window for burgers and massive ice cream menu
  • Pan’s Pizza – Take-out pizza joint in town center
  • Blue Paddle Bistro – Cozy bistro with upscale farm-to-table menu

Markets & Shops

  • Keeler’s Bay Variety – Full-service local grocery with butcher, wine, and essentials
  • Arbor Farm Market – Local produce, dairy, baked goods, and small gifts
  • The Green Frog – Gift shop with Vermont-made crafts and specialty foods
  • Viva Marketplace – Local goods + cider donuts + used book nook
  • Champlain Islands Candy Lab – Handmade truffles, caramels, and espresso drinks
  • Island Craft Shop – Seasonal co-op for handmade art and crafts

Outdoor & Family Activities

  • Allenholm Farm – Petting zoo, apples, creemees, and wagon rides
  • Fox Hill Trail – Short hike with panoramic lake views
  • Landon Nature Trail – Easy wooded loop behind Folsom School
  • South Hero Rec Park – Playground, StoryWalk trail, sports fields
  • Sand Bar State Park – Shallow sandy beach ideal for kids
  • White’s Beach – Peaceful public swim spot with sunset views
  • Island Line Trail Access – Bike Causeway with summer ferry
  • Bike + Kayak Rentals – Available at Allenholm Farm and Hero’s Welcome

Wineries & Breweries

  • Snow Farm Vineyard – Lakeview wine tastings and live summer concerts
  • Two Heroes Brewery – Small-batch craft beer with a full pub menu

Grand Isle

Markets & Farm Stands

  • Pomykala Farm – Self-serve stand with seasonal veggies and flowers
  • Dreamwalker Farm – Eggs, pork, beef, and local goods (Th–Sun)
  • Grand Isle Country Store – Quick stop for groceries and sandwiches

Farmers Market

  • Grand Isle Farmers Market – Saturdays 10–2 from May to October

Outdoor & Family Activities

  • Grand Isle State Park – Shore access, playground, nature programs, and trail
  • Ironwood Trail – Easy loop trail inside state park
  • Dead Creek Marshland – Birdwatching hotspot near southern edge of island

Wineries

  • Ellison Estate Vineyard – Organic wines with summer tastings Fridays and Saturdays

North Hero

Restaurants

  • Hero’s Welcome – Iconic general store with deli, bakery, and picnic tables
  • Two Heroes Grill – Casual outdoor grill with tiki bar energy (summer only)
  • North Hero House – Upscale lakeside dining with New England charm
  • Shore Acres / Bravo Zulu – Inside fine dining or cocktails on the deck with water views (two restaurants)
  • Pier Bar

Shops

  • Hero’s Welcome General Store – Everything from books to wine to souvenirs

Outdoor & Family Activities

  • Knight Point State Park – Beach, playground, picnic area, and short trails
  • Pelots Point Trail – Easy nature trail through forest and marsh
  • Viewpoint Park – Scenic overlook of Lake Champlain and New York side

Breweries

  • Kraemer & Kin

Isle La Motte

Restaurants

  • Ruthcliffe Lodge & Restaurant – Dinner on the lake with Italian-inspired menu

Wineries

  • Isle La Motte Vineyard – Casual tasting room open weekends in summer

Burlington

Breweries/Cideries

  • Citizen Cider- Burlington, VT- great cider and outdoor seating
  • Foam Brewers- Burlington, VT- great beer and outdoor seating
  • Fiddlehead- Shelburne, VT
  • Burlington Beer Company
  • Zero Gravity- Burlington, VT- great beer and outdoor seating

Restaurants

  • Hen of the Wood- Burlington, VT- upscale farm-to-table dining with a great bar
  • Honey Road- Burlington, VT- Mediterranean-inspired small plates and cocktails
  • Single Pebble- Burlington, VT- Chinese food with a great bar
  • Dedalus- Burlington, VT- upscale dining with a great bar
  • Poppy Café- lunch sandwiches
  • Café Hot- Breakfast spot

Brewery Guide

  • Hill Farmstead- Greensboro Bend, VT- arguably the Mecca of beer in the entire US.
  • 1st Republic- Essex Junction, VT
  • Black Flannel- Essex Junction, VT
  • Von Trapp- Stowe, VT- extremely cool bier hall style with outdoor seating and vibes
  • Alchemist- Stowe, VT- one of the top breweries in the country
